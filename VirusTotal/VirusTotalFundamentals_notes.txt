VirusTotal is a website that scans files, webpages, domains, and IPs, against a large 
number of antivirus scanners. This allows a user to get feedback from a large number 
of sources and helps them determine if something is known to be malicious or known 
to be benign.

Files and URLs can be uploaded to VirusTotal. 
⚠️However, once they are uploaded, they should be considered public.⚠️
Organizations that pay for access can download uploaded files. 
As a result, do not upload sensitive files or files that belong to your employer.
